# EduEase App (Base44)

This project was built using Base44.
[View the live app here] (https://simplifi-copy-e00d310c.base44.app)

The EduEase app is  an AI-enabled digital learning and assignment-helper app. The app simplifies lesssons for students at any level, starting from the primary level to the tertiary level, in such a way that the student gains a good understanding of the lesson from any subject of choice. The app also helps the student in tackling his or her assignments. 
The app accepts written or voice prompts and gives response based on the input.
